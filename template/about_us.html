This tool was inspired by a blog post by @david_a_eaves and created during some late night hacking at the Super Happy Hacker House #1 by @lukec and @kevinj.

VanTrash is a service, but also a case study designed to show city councils the services, resources and ingenuity that can emerge when cities open up and share their data.
